Vision

The vision of AIE is to provide world-class education and to be part with international standards, in order to prepare Nepalese students to sit for the international preparation courses. More than international standard our vision is to prepare a Nepali student for global competition with high quality education around the globe. Nepalese students must learn new languages and also acquire basic and advanced IT skills in order to meet the demands of newly industrialized organizations and be eligible for selection at foreign colleges and universities for higher education. To assist all AIE students to attain their personal, educational and career goals quickly and efficiently with the aid of the latest technological advances and excellent counselling services for abroad study is the main vision of our organisation.

Code Of Conduct

Code of Conduct: Ample International Education Pvt. Ltd. The following code of conduct to ensures our quality services to our international partners (college/universities) abroad, students and our other parties: - Represent institutions abroad with honesty and integrity. - Strive to understand represented institutions' mission and goals, and approach to education. - Become familiar with represented institutions admission prerequisites, English language proficiency requirements, application and acceptance procedures, and current objectives. - Provide accurate and up-to-date information on represented institutions academic programs, living arrangements, tuition and fees, refund policies, and student support services. - Provide to prospective students accurate and realistic advising in keeping with their educational objectives and financial situation. - Provide all of represented institutions literature to prospective students free of charge. - Use appropriate opportunities to increase public awareness of represented institutions. - Include parent or sponsors in the advising process whenever possible. - Demonstrate respect for the educational, economic, familial, cultural, and political background of prospective students. - Not knowingly participate in, nor condone unfair or discriminatory practices. - Provide competent, reputable, and professional service to prospective students to protect the interest of the students, represented institutions and our company. - Represent all institutions fairly to enable prospective students and their families to make informed decisions. - Cooperate in reasonable and appropriate ways with other educational consultants for the benefit of the students and represented institutions. - Refrain from making any statements that are misleading, open to misinterpretations or not true to prospective students or their families during the advising process. - Abide by any and all federal and state laws governing the consultation process and fair business practices.
